fort-2-jul
https://github.com/WalidOEa/Fort-2-Jul

World's first novel Fortran 77 to Julia transpiler that maps a significant anount of the Fortran domain to the Julia sub-domain.

Completed in accordance of my dissertation for my undergraduate degree at the University of Southampton.

tetrecs
https://github.com/WalidOEa/Tetrecs

A colour matching game inspired by the classic game Tetris. In addition to the singleplayer, Tetrecs offers a multiplayer mode that utilises Java websockets to create seamless server-client connections hosted on my very own homeserver.

fake tweet detector
https://github.com/WalidOEa/Fake-Tweet-Detector

An extension that identifies real and fake tweets with up to 90% detection accuracy utilising a powerful ML model trained and tested on the MediaEval2015 dataset.

tile language
https://github.com/WalidOEa/TileDSL

Utilising Happy and Alex, TileDSL is a Domain Specific Language for the manipulation of tiles to create cool and interesting patterns.
